Is Hawai'i diverse when compared to the rest of the USA?

From the table and chart above about the population numbers for the races in Hawai'i, one can find the conclusion that Hawai'i is diverse with having more Asians than others. But there are many different races in the Asian race which further adds to the diversity.

From the table and chart above about the population numbers for the languages spoken in Hawai'i, one can find the conclusion that Hawai'i is majority an English speaking state with an Asian or Pacific language as the next.

From the table and chart above about the population numbers for the races in USA. One can conclude that the most dominant race is White at 42.7%. The next dominant race would be Hispanic or Latino.

From the table and chart above about the population numbers for the languages spoken in USA. One can conclude from the table and chart that the most spoken language is English at 78.1%. The next most spoken language is Spanish at 13.4% and the other languages falling in the single digits.

The left chart compares the percentages of Race from Hawai'i versus USA. The right chart chart compares the percentages of language spoken from Hawai'i versus USA. From the left chart, one can see Hawai'i has a higher percentage of Asians compared to USA. While USA has more percentage of White. Hawai'i has more percentages of Two or more races and Native Hawaiian/Pacific Island which shows that Hawai'i is more diverse than USA. From the right chart, both Hawai'i and USA can be seen as having English as the majority language. But Hawai'i has more percentage in the Asian language compared to USA.
